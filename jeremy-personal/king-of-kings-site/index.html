Who We Are...

**The King of Kings Who Started with One Woman of God: Grace Nwoye – A Legacy of Miracles and Testimonies** In the beginning, it was just one woman—**Grace Nwoye**—a devoted servant of God, chosen and anointed by the **King of kings**. She stepped out in faith, carrying a divine mandate to preach the Gospel, heal the sick, and set the captives free. What started as a small seed of obedience has now grown into a mighty tree, with countless testimonies of God’s power radiating from her ministry. **The Humble Beginnings** Sister Grace Nwoye was not a name known to many at first. She was simply a woman sold out to God, praying in her closet, interceding for souls, and trusting in the Lord’s promises. Like Deborah, Esther, and Hannah, she stood firm in her faith, believing that what God had spoken over her life would come to pass. Through her unwavering faith, miracles began to happen. The sick were healed, the barren conceived, and the oppressed were delivered. Word spread, and soon, people from different nations began to seek her prayers and prophetic ministrations. **The Explosion of Testimonies** Today, the name **Grace Nwoye** is synonymous with divine encounters. The testimonies are endless: - **Healings:** Cancer vanishing, the blind seeing, and the lame walking—all by the power of Jesus Christ. - **Deliverances:** Demonic chains broken, addicts set free, and families restored. - **Financial Breakthroughs:** Those who were once in lack now testifying of supernatural provision. - **Miraculous Conceptions:** Women who were declared barren now holding their babies, declaring God’s faithfulness. **A Movement, Not Just a Ministry** What began with one woman has now become a global movement. Churches, prayer groups, and ministries have been birthed out of her teachings and miracles. The **King of kings** has used her mightily to show that He is the same yesterday, today, and forever (Hebrews 13:8). **The Lesson for Us All** Grace Nwoye’s story teaches us that: 1. **God uses the willing, not just the "qualified."** 2. **A single act of faith can spark a revival.** 3. **Testimonies inspire more testimonies—when we share what God has done, others believe for their own miracles.** Today, because of one woman’s **yes** to God, nations are being transformed. The **King of kings** is still in the business of doing the impossible, and the name **Grace Nwoye** stands as a living testament to His power. **What is your testimony? What will God do through *your* obedience?** The same God who started with Grace Nwoye can start with *you*—and multiply it beyond imagination. **To God be all the glory!
